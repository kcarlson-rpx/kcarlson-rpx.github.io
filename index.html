Welcome to RPX Insurance Tools

This page collects some of the applications that RPX Insurance uses to help in underwriting and better serving our clients. You can access the standalone applications by clicking on the application name below.



The UW Dashboard collects information on clients/prospects from Salesforce applications, ecosystems, and FLRs, and our policy log.



The FLR Dashboard shows more in-depth information about FLRs, including various tech descriptions and all defendants.



The Ecosystem Tool is used to create the "ecosystem slide" from our client-facing decks and shows more informationa about the ecosystem.



The Benchmark Tool is used to create the "benchmark slide" from our client-facing decks.
